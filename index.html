Valbella needed a way to connect with their existing audience and convert them ahead of key trading periods. With a small delivery radius and provincial licensing limiting them to Alberta, reaching the customers they already had was everything. The idea started as a Christmas magazine to drive pre-orders, but BBQ season is Valbella's busiest period and we wanted to capitalise on that too. The magazine grew into a seasonal series, telling the brand's story, showcasing products in context, and giving customers a reason to buy.
Creative Direction · Content Planning · Copywriting & Editing · Interviews · Graphic Design & Layout · Photoshoot Coordination · Project Management
I built the magazine entirely from scratch, developing the concept, planning the content calendar, conducting interviews with the team, writing and editing all copy, and designing the layout through to print-ready files. 6,000 copies were printed and distributed across two editions. For the summer edition I also organised and coordinated a professional BBQ photoshoot, creating original imagery used throughout the magazine and across wider marketing campaigns.
Valbella had a record-breaking Christmas, surpassing the previous year's orders. The Christmas edition, tracked via UTM-tagged QR codes embedded in the print, showed a 10% conversion rate from scan to purchase, a strong return for a print piece. Customer feedback was brilliant throughout. The summer edition was released as I finished my time at Valbella, with the original photography continuing to be used across wider marketing campaigns.
Studio 67 is a licensed art gallery offering pop art by day and absinthe by night, and was starting from scratch. Before opening, they needed a brand identity, website, social presence, and a launch strategy that would connect them with their local community and build awareness from the ground up.
Branding · Website Development · Content Creation · Launch Strategy · Social Media · Campaign Management · PR Agency Coordination · AI-assisted Redesign · Remote Project Management
I built the marketing foundations for the launch: branding, website, social presence, content, and launch strategy. To build community awareness I initiated and ran Studio 67's Independent Business campaign: the owner painted five local independent businesses, gifting one painting each month. I posted each reveal to Studio 67's social channels using Instagram's collaboration feature to expand reach and grow the audience. I also attended networking events and initiated giveaways to boost awareness. I initiated the relationship with a PR agency, and later led a full website redesign while managing the project remotely from Canada, using AI-assisted development to improve the customer experience and explore emerging tools in a real business environment.
The Independent Business campaign achieved an average post engagement rate of 16.3%, surpassing the industry average of 0.87% by over 18 times. The campaign created a buzz within the community and caught the eye of a local magazine. The PR relationship I initiated helped gather content for the redesign, and the website is currently in the final stages of sign off and will be going live shortly.
